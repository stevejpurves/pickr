About pick this

pick this was built by Ben Bougher, Jacob Foshee, Evan Bianco, and a combination of Chris Chalcraft and Matt Hall at the 2014 Geophysics Hackathon in Denver, Colorado. The demo version we built at that event had a hard-coded image and no voting, but the basic concept was all there.

Since the hackathon, Ben, Matt, and Evan have continued to develop the app. After what an uncharitable person might call 'JavaScript flailing', the shockingly talented Steve Purves stepped in to provide a drawing interface that is actually pretty awesome. Thank you Steve!

FAQ

How does the reputation system work? You start with 1 point. You receive 3 points for an interpretation, or for uploading an image. You receive 1 point for each upvote your interpretations get, and lose a point for a downvote. You need 15 points before you can upload.

Why can't I upload an image? You probably don't have enough rep: you need 15 reputation points before you can upload.

Can I re-use these images? Maybe. Check them permissions in the info box below the image. It should state whether or not the image has an open license. If it does, you can re-use it. If not, then you need permission from the image owner, who should be given in the info box.
