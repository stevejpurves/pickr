About pick this

pick this was built by Ben Bougher, Jacob Foshee, Evan Bianco, and a combination of Chris Chalcraft and Matt Hall at the 2014 Geophysics Hackathon in Denver, Colorado. The demo version we built at that event had a hard-coded image and no voting, but the basic concept was all there.

Since the hackathon, Ben, Matt, and Evan have continued to develop the app. After what an uncharitable person might call 'JavaScript flailing', the shockingly talented Steve Purves stepped in to provide a drawing interface that is actually pretty awesome. Thank you Steve!

Support this project!

We want pick this to be free to use. But we have a long list of enhancements to add and a short list of bugs to fix. If you would like to donate time or money to the project, please get in touch.
